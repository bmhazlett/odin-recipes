Viking Lamb

Description

Leg of a lamb baked in a hole in the ground.

Ingredients

  • 1 leg of lamb
  • Honey
  • Mustard
  • Cloves of garlic, sliced inserted into pockets
  • Sprigs of rosemary
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Aluminum Foil

Directions

  1. Insert sliced garlic into pockets
  2. Cover leg of lambda with honey and mustard
  3. Salt and pepper the leg of lamb
  4. Put rosemary over the lamb
  5. Wrap lamb in foil
  6. Dig cooking pit about half a meter
  7. Fill hole with gocks and wood and light the wood on fire
  8. Wait for rocks to glow red hot
  9. Remove rocks and add lamb
  10. Re-fil pit with dirt
  11. After about 1 hour and 15 minutes remove lamb and enjoy
