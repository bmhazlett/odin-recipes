Mead

Description

A sweet honey wine.

Ingredients

  • 30 to 35 liters of water
  • 5kg to 10gk clover honey
  • 1 sachet of bakers yeast
  • Milton Mix

Directions

  1. Fill a boiler with water and bring to a boil
  2. Reduce heat and add honey
  3. Remove froth into a container
  4. Second boiler is boiled for 10 minutes
  5. Scoop out 3 cups of mix and add to second boiler
  6. When small batch is around 24 degree C add sachet of yeast
  7. Stir with whisk
  8. When honey mix is cooled, add it to the water in the barrel
  9. When barrel is at 24C add yeast mix
  10. Screw th elid on the barrel and add air trap
